Letting \(\displaystyle G(x)=\int_5^x \sqrt{t+1}\, dt\text{,}\) we have
\begin{align*} F(x) \amp=\int_{x}^5 \sqrt{t+1}\, dt \\ \amp = -\int_5^x\sqrt{t+1}\, dt\\ \amp = -G(x) \end{align*}
and hence
\begin{align*} F'(x) \amp= -G'(x) \\ \amp= -\sqrt{x+1} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/th_FTC_I.html}{\text{FTC I}}: G'(x)=\sqrt{x+1})\text{.} \end{align*}
