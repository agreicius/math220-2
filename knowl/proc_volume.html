Procedure 1.9.2. Volume via cross sections.

To compute the volume of a solid region \(\mathcal{S}\) via \(x\)-cross sections, proceed as follows:
  1. Sketch \(\mathcal{S}\) along with a typical cross section \(\mathcal{S}_x\text{.}\)
  2. Derive a formula for \(A(x)\) in terms of \(x\text{.}\)
  3. Determine the appropriate limits of integration: \(x=a\) and \(x=b\text{.}\)
  4. Compute \(\displaystyle \int_a^b A(x)\, dx\text{.}\)
