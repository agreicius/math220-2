Consider the somewhat tricky substitution: \(u=2^{\cos 2^x}\text{,}\) \(du=-(\ln 2)^2\sin 2^x\cdot 2^x\cdot 2^{\cos 2^x} dx\text{.}\) We then have
\begin{align*} \int_{0}^\pi \sin(2^x)2^{\cos(2^x)+x}\, dx \amp = -\frac{1}{(\ln 2)^2}\int_{2^{\cos 1}}^{2^{\cos 2^\pi}} du \\ \amp = -\frac{1}{(\ln 2)^2}u\Bigr\vert_{2^{\cos 1}}^{2^{\cos 2^\pi}}\\ \amp = \frac{1}{(\ln 2)^2}(2^{\cos 1}-2^{\cos 2^\pi})\text{.} \end{align*}
