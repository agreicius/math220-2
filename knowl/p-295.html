A function \(f\) is one-to-one on its domain \(D\) if \(f(x_1)\ne f(x_2)\) for all \(x_1, x_2\in D\) with \(x_1\ne x_2\text{.}\) We express this with logical notation as
\begin{equation*} x_1\ne x_2\implies f(x_1)\ne f(x_2)\text{,} \end{equation*}
or equivalently, using the contrapositive,
\begin{equation*} f(x_1)=f(x_2)\implies x_1=x_2\text{.} \end{equation*}
