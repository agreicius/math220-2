Solution 1.12.10.1.

  1. We have
    \begin{align*} f'(x) \amp = (\ln \sin x)'e^{\cos x}+\ln\sin x(e^{\cos x})'\\ \amp = \frac{\cos x}{\sin x}e^{\cos x}+\ln\sin x \cdot e^{\cos x}(-\sin x)\\ \amp = e^{\cos x}(\cot x-\sin x\ln \sin x)\text{.} \end{align*}
  2. We have
    \begin{align*} f'(x) \amp = \frac{(2^x+3^{x^2})'}{\ln 3(2^x+3^{x^2})}\\ \amp = \frac{\ln 2\cdot 2^x+2\ln 3 \cdot x 3^{x^2}}{\ln 3(2^x+3^{x^2})}\text{.} \end{align*}
