Decide whether the following limit expressions have determinate or indeterminate forms. If determinate, compute the limit.
  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0+}\frac{\sin x}{\ln x}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{x}{e^{-x}}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\lim_{x\to (\pi/2)^-}\frac{\tan x}{\cos x}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{e^x}{2^x+3^x}\)
