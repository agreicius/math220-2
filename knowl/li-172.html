Item 6.

We have
\begin{align} \ln(ab)\amp =\ln a+\ln b, \text{ for all \(a,b\in (0,\infty)\). }\tag{1.11.3}\\ \ln(a/b)\amp =\ln a-\ln b, \text{ for all \(a,b\in (0,\infty)\), \(b\ne 0\). }\tag{1.11.4}\\ \ln a^r\amp =r\ln a, \text{ for all \(a\in (0,\infty)\) and \(r\in \R\). }\tag{1.11.5} \end{align}
