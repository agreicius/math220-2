Let \(n\) be a positive integer.
  1. \(\sum\limits_{k=1}^n c=cn\) for any \(c\in \R\text{.}\)
  2. \(\sum\limits_{k=1}^nk=\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\text{.}\)
  3. \(\sum\limits_{k=1}^nk^2=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}\text{.}\)
  4. \(\sum\limits_{k=1}^nk^3=\left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^2\text{.}\)
