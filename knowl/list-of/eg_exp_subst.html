Example 1.17.4. Exponential substitution.

Compute \(\displaystyle\int \frac{1}{e^x+e^{-x}}\, dx\text{.}\)
Solution.
We have
\begin{align*} \int \frac{1}{e^x+e^{-x}}\, dx \amp = \int \frac{1}{u+1/u}\cdot \frac{1}{u}\, du \amp (u=e^x, du=e^x dx\implies dx=\frac{1}{u}du) \\ \amp = \int \frac{1}{u^2+1}\, du\\ \amp = \arctan(e^x)+C\text{.} \end{align*}
