Example 1.17.2. Vertex form.

Compute \(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x^2-6x+18}\, dx\text{.}\)
Solution.
We have
\begin{align*} \int \frac{1}{x^2-6x+18}\, dx \amp = \int \frac{1}{(x-3)^2+9}\, dx \\ \amp = \frac{1}{9}\int\frac{1}{((x-3)/3)^2+1}\\ \amp =\frac{1}{3}\int \frac{1}{u^2+1} \amp (u=(x-3)/3, du=\frac{1}{3}dx)\\ \amp = \arctan((x-3)/3)+C\text{.} \end{align*}
