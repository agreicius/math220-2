Example 1.19.7. Odd tangent power.

Compute \(\displaystyle \int \tan^5 x\sec^7 x\, dx\text{.}\)
Solution.
Peel off a factor of \(\tan x\sec x\) and use the substitution \(u=\sec x\text{,}\) \(du=\sec x\tan x\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} \int \tan^5 x\sec^7 x\, dx \amp = \int (\sec^2-1)^2\sec^6 x \, \underset{du}{\underbrace{\sec x\tan x\, dx}}\\ \amp = \int u^{10}-2u^{8}+u^6\, du \amp (u=\sec x, du=\sec x\tan x dx)\\ \amp = \frac{1}{11}\sec^{11}x-\frac{2}{9}\sec^9 x+\frac{1}{7}\sec x+C\text{.} \end{align*}
