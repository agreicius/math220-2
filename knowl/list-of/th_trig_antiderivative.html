Theorem 1.11.6. Trigonometric antiderivatives.

  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\int \tan x\, dx=-\ln\lvert \cos x\rvert+C=\ln\lvert\sec x\rvert+C\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\int \cot x\, dx=\ln\vert \sin x\vert+C\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\int \sec x\, dx=\ln\vert \sec x+\tan x\vert+C\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\int \csc x\, dx=-\ln\vert \csc x+\cot x\vert+C\)
