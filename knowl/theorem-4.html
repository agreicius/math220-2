Theorem 1.3.2. Summation formulas.

Let \(n\) be a positive integer.
  1. \(\displaystyle \sum\limits_{k=1}^n 1=n\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \sum\limits_{k=1}^nk=\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \sum\limits_{k=1}^nk^2=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}\)
  4. \(\sum\limits_{k=1}^nk^3=\left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^2\text{.}\)
